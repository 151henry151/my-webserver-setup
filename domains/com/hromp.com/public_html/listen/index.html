Background audio recording with automatic segment rotation.

Continuously capture ambient audio from your surroundings and maintain a configurable rolling buffer of recordings. Perfect for reviewing recent conversations, sounds, or audio events.

Key Features

🎙️ Continuous Recording

Runs silently in the background, capturing audio 24/7 with minimal battery impact.

⚙️ Configurable Segments

Adjustable recording segment duration from 15 seconds to 30 minutes.

🔄 Rotating Buffer

Automatically manages storage by deleting old segments and adding new ones.

🔒 Privacy-First

All recordings stored locally on device, no cloud uploads or network access required.

⏯️ Instant Playback

Access any moment from your audio history with just a few taps.

🔋 Battery Optimized

Efficient background processing designed to minimize battery drain.

How It Works

1

Background Service

A persistent background service continuously captures audio from your device microphone.

2

Segmented Recording

Audio is divided into user-configurable time segments (e.g., 1-minute chunks).

3

Rotating Buffer

When retention period is reached, oldest segments are automatically deleted and replaced.

4

Always Available

At any moment, you have access to the complete audio history within your retention window.
